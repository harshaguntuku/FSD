About Us

Andhra University is a prestigious educational institution located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 1926, it is one of the oldest universities in India and has played a significant role in the development of higher education in the country.Our college is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a culture of innovation and excellence.

It was founded in 1926 by Dr. C. R. Reddy, who was its first Vice-Chancellor. The university was initially established as a residential teaching-cum-affiliating university and has grown significantly over the decades.

Andhra University offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. The university has several faculties, including Arts, Science, Engineering, Law, Pharmacy, Management, and more. It has been accredited with an "A+" grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).
